Solar Energy App

This smartphone application enables users to manage domestic solar panels. Aimed at homeowners, this app allows for solar panel power management, energy consumption statistics, as real-time data visualisations.

The problem

The issue was discovered when some individuals were turning their solar system on and off manually. After conducting research in this area to see how an app might offer them a practical solution. Numerous applications that offer insights into solar energy are easily found in the market; nevertheless, they do not grant customers complete control over turning on and off their solar energy system or their home's main electricity. With the use of this app, customers may turn on their solar energy when the sun comes out, which turns off the main electricity.

User Personas

Task Scenario

  1. Emily’s house has a solar panel, and she is doing a manual task to switch it ON when sunlight comes and switch OFF it when sunlight is gone. She wants an app in which she can operate it with a single click, even if she is outside the house.
  2. She downloaded the PowerMonitor app from the app store and launched the app on her smartphone.
  3. After that, she started to create a profile on it and fill in all the personal details in the app to set up their account.
  4. After setting up the profile and filling out all the personal details, she got the option to connect the solar system with this app, and then she started the solar integration process step by step.
  5. She followed all the steps to integrate the solar system with the app and got the confirmation message “solar connected”.
  6. After these steps, she can check their solar energy production and consumed energy on the user dashboard, and she can easily switch solar power with main electricity with only one click.
  7. She can customise the notification options in the app and get notified according to them.

Future Improvements

The development of the project does not end here, based on the responses and suggestions from the users during user testing, it highlights the significance of continuing revisions to develop a user-centric product that meets the user's needs and preferences.

This app has many options to enhance its functionality for users. It is an energy management app, and numerous features can be integrated with it, such as power control for home appliances, cost savings, and monitoring electricity bills. Apart from that, some educational elements and gamification elements can encourage users to use this app. Educational elements can help them understand the environmental impacts of their energy choices. With gamification, some energy conservation challenges and rewards can motivate more users to adopt sustainable energy.

Several ethical considerations and permissions are necessary to ensure compliance with relevant regulations and standards when developing this solar energy app in the real world. These include data privacy and security, accuracy and reliability, accessibility and inclusivity, environmental impact, and ethical use of data.
